    RESEARCH









    The HKUST-NIE Social Media Lab. focuses on research and design of next generation social media systems and networks for emerging applications in future cyber-physical environments and societies



    • media broadcast in human-mobile networks;

    • out-of-home interactive and sensing digital signages;

    • smartphones and portable/wearable devices connected cloud-computing;

    • multi-modal sensing/actuation assisted living, health-care and environmental monitoring platforms;
