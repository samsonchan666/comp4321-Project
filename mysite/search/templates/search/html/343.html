About this site




This is a one-stop portal where you can find all HKUST\'s publications and newsletters digitized and preserved in the Digital University Archives. In addition to the archival copies, it also contains current issues downloaded from the University and departmental websites.


The database provides a rich source of information about the University, schools and departments, as well as student and alumni activities. You can browse by department or office, or search the content by keywords. Whether you are searching for current information or want to look for information in publications from HKUST\'s early days, this database is worth a visit.
