Introduction


With the 25th anniversary of HKUST, the Department of Computer and Engineering
(CSE) launches the Alumni Affairs Committee with the goal to build close
ties between alumni and the department. With the collaboration from the CSE Alumni Association, the CSE
Alumni Family Homecoming Day 2015 will be held on 22-Nov 2015 (Sun) for
alumni, faculty, students and staff to mingle. Our department head, Prof. Qiang YANG will update alumni the latest
development of HKUST and CSE. Family members of alumni are also encouraged
to join "Hour of Code" sessions and afternoon tea party
together.

Hour of Code Sessions


2015-11-17 Update: We merge the two
Scratch sessions "Junior Scratch" and "Advanced Scratch" into one Scratch
session "Scratch Programming".



We will have two parallel sessions of hour of code in CSE Lab 1 and Lab
4 respectively. It will be conducted in English supplemented with
Cantonese.




  • CSE Lab 1 (Rm4221):
    Scratch Programming for children 6-12 who want to learn
    some Scratch or hone their Scratch skills;

  • CSE Lab 4 (Rm4210):
    Android Mobile App for children 12+,
    participants are encouraged to bring Android devices to the workshop (to
    try out their app immediately), and it\'s going to be fun!


We will require children to be accompanied by their parents in the
sessions.
