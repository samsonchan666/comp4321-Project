Events & Programs

The Center for the Arts (CFA) organizes a wide variety of programs to promote the appreciation and participation of students in artistic and cultural activities. We hold exhibitions, concerts, film shows, seminars, workshops, and arrange visits and many
more to serve the art-loving HKUST community.
