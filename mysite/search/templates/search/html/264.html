About SyMLab


The HKUST-DT System and Media Laboratory(SyMLab) locates in HKUST, partially funded by Deutsche Telekom and in close cooperation with Telekom Innovation Laboratories(T-labs).


Our current research interests are Mobile Augmented Reality (AR) and AR content generation, mobile computing, and social network.

Our Team


Currently we have one professor, three postdocs, seven PhD students and two Mphil students. We have multiple openings for postdoctoral researchers and research assistants on building a cloud-based Mobile Augmented Reality platform and other interesting mobile computing research topics.
