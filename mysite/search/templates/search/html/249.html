About Us


Division of Integrative Systems and Design has been established in November 2017 to push the forefront of transdisciplinary research and experiential learning both within HKUST and on the global stage.


With ISD, we will stop entrenching students into singular discipline. Instead we will further HKUSTs aspirations in readying new innovators to tackle complex problems of the real world. President, HKUST, 2018.


The ISD undergraduate degree program, aims to nurture innovators and researchers with design and systems thinking, will be launched in September 2018. The MPhil program in Technology, Leadership and Entrepreneurship will also continue to be offered as one of the ISD education programs.
