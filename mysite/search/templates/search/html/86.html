    Generous Support for Research Postgraduate Studies


    Our priority is not only to provide the best education and research opportunities, but also the means for that to be achieved. Financial concerns should not be a hurdle for you to pursue your dreams.




















    Postgraduate Studentship



    • HK$207,960/year (~US$26,600/year)

    • Up to 2 years for full-time MPhil students and 4 years for full-time PhD students




    Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme



    • A prestigious award for the best PhD students around the world

    • Stipend of HK$301,200 (~US$38,600) per year for 4 years

    • Excellent Research Award of HK$40,000 (~US$5,000) in the first year of study

    • Conference Travel Allowance of HK$12,600 (~US$1,600)per year for4 years




    Asian Future Leaders Scholarship Program



    • A scholarship for new students from East, Southeast and South Asia to pursue postgraduate studies and cross-cultural enrichment




    Accomodation



    • Guaranteed university accommodation for the first 2 years of your full-time research postgraduate studies
