Want to Hire Graduates/Interns?


Home /
/













Thank you for your interest in hiring from HKUST. Career Center can help meet your recruitment needs through a wide range of employer services. We would also like to collect feedback from employers for continuous improvementon the academic curriculum and learning experience for our students. Please read on to find out how we can be of help to you or collaborate in one way or the other.


Services available to help you recruit the right candidates



  • Free portals for posting job advertisements

    HKUST Job Board


    The Job Board of Career Center is one of the best options if you want to recruit HKUST students. You may sign up for an account and post jobs online. To start, please click here.
    \r


    Joint Institutions Job Information System (JIJIS)


    It is a shared job site for the eight universities in Hong Kong. Registered employers can post job vacancy information online directly for current students\' or fresh graduates\' browsing. If you target HKUST students only, you can choose to display the job information exclusively to HKUST students. Jobs posted include full-time employment, part-time employment, temporary employment, summer employment and internship. Moreover, you may have access to applicants\' CV database online and select suitable candidates for your openings.

  • On-Campus Recruitment Events

    Recruitment Talks


    Career Center organizes on-campus recruitment talks from September to April every year. They provide excellent opportunities for HKUST students to meet prospective employers face-to-face.If you are interested in holding recruitment talks on our campus, please fill out the Recruitment Talk Request Form. We would help you identify the most effective channel to meet your recruitment needs.
    \r


    Career Mosaic


    Career Mosaic is a career fair organized by Career Center, usually held in September/October and February/March. The objectives are to provide a platform for corporate branding and on-campus recruitment, as well as enhance HKUST students\' awareness of the career opportunities offered by promising employers. If you are interested in this event, please fill out the Career Mosaic Enquiry Form and our staff will contact you shortly.

  • Off-Campus Recruitment Events

    We can help you disseminate the information to HKUST students through our website.

  • On-Campus Selection Exercises

    Upon request and subject to venue availability, we can provide facilities for you to conduct on-campus selection exercises, such as written tests, group discussions, interviews, etc.

  • Candidate Shortlist Announcements

    We can make shortlist announcements for you through the Career Center website if adequate lead time is given.

  • Local/Mainland/Overseas Internships

    We would be happy to help you recruit summer/winter/part-time/year-out interns for your local, mainland or overseas office(s). For more information, pleaseclick here.
