Introduction of BDI













Introduction


About Us

Leveraging on the existing strength and resources of the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), the Big Data Institute (BDI) was established upon the needs from both industry and society for a
new model for managing multi-disciplinary focal points for Big Data research, by coordinating facultys research on Big Data and Data Science at HKUST. BDI will provide a strong and highly visible leadership role in Big Data and
Data Science research in Hong Kong and the world.
